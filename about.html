About IE Travel Blogger

Sharing travel stories, tips, and inspiration for every type of traveler

Hello, I'm Jane!

Welcome to IE Travel Blogger, a place where I share my passion for exploring the world and helping others create unforgettable travel experiences.

My journey began in 2015 when I took a leap of faith, quit my corporate job, and embarked on what was supposed to be a one-year trip around the world. That year turned into a lifestyle of slow travel, remote work, and a deep appreciation for the diverse cultures, landscapes, and people our planet has to offer.

Over the past 8 years, I've visited 50+ countries across 6 continents, experiencing everything from budget backpacking through Southeast Asia to luxury resorts in the Maldives, solo adventures in South America to family trips across Europe.

What makes IE Travel Blogger different is that I don't believe in one-size-fits-all travel advice. Whether you're a budget backpacker, a luxury vacationer, a solo adventurer, a digital nomad, or traveling with family, you'll find content tailored to your travel style and needs.

Through this blog, I aim to:

  • Share honest, detailed accounts of my travel experiences
  • Provide practical tips and comprehensive guides for destinations worldwide
  • Help you travel smarter, whether you're on a shoestring budget or seeking luxury experiences
  • Inspire you to step out of your comfort zone and discover the transformative power of travel

When I'm not on the road, you can find me in my home base of Dublin, Ireland, planning my next adventure, testing travel gear, or enjoying a good book with a cup of coffee.

Thank you for joining me on this journey. I hope the stories and tips shared here inspire your own adventures!
